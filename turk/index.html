Instructions

Place a marker at the specified location for each column and answer image-related questions:

  1. Finding the head of the biological specimen, move mouse to the location over the image in the first column.
  2. Annotate the location by clicking the left mouse. If the head/tail is out of frame, select the "Out of Frame" option.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for the tail of the specimen in the second column.
  4. Determine the z direction of the column in column 3. If you are unsure of the direction, choose "Not Confident".
  5. In the last column, select the level of confidence with your identification of the head and tail, given the three options.
